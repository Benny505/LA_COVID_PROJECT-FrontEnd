Overall.

How has Covid19 affected the overall well being of my family? Are we just getting by or is it more of a 10/10 would quarantine again?

Family:

As I said, my family is very fortunate in these times. Aside from stress and less social time we have been almost enjoying this break from normality

Pros:

  • less school-related stress
  • more time to do the things I enjoy
  • more time to spend with famiy
  • a fun experience and divergence from normality

Cons:

  • less social time
  • disc golf events being (mostly) called off
  • less productive things to fill the day with
  • less access to the outside world

Overall:

Even though quarantine has brought about a lot of enjoyable new habits and opurtunities, I hope something like this doesn't happen again. Being couped up in the house with nothing to do can become maddening and even though I don't talk much, it is getting old talking to the same three people (and only them) every day. That and the fact that a lot of disc golf events are cancelled makes me say that even though I did enjoy it (and still kind of am), I will feel refreshed the day that it ends.
