My Covid Experience
Family

Covid19 has harmed many families. It is putting people out of jobs and straining their mental stamina. But how have these events affected my family?

School

With school moving online many there has been quite the spread of opinions. Many find it harder to connect with teachers and friends, some are dissapointed about grad being cancelled. Others however, have a very different set of opinions.

Hobbies

Hobbies are likely one of the only sectors that have been mostly affected positively by covid. With all of this spare time people are scrambling to try and fill it.

Learning

School isn't the only place where I learn (thank goodness). With all of my extra time I've been trying to spend at least some of it wisely.

Exercise

Trying to get exercise during covid is possibly one of the most difficult parts of it. People have been calling the weight gain the Covid 15. Have I been able to avoid it?

Overall

How has Covid19 affected the overall well being of my family? Are we just getting by or is it more of a 10/10 would quarantine again?

What is This Place?

This website was created to share one quarantine experience and give others the option to share their own. Throughout you will find all important/interesting aspects of my family's last couple months in quarantine. You may be wondering why it was mentioned that others can share their experiences. If you look to the left of this paragraph you will see a text box that allows you to submit a name (optional) and some text. This particular one is just an example but there are others around the website (these boxes are toatally optional to write in. I made them mostly because I have too much time on my hands). PLEASE NOTE: this website was designed to be viewed on a laptop/computer (not a phone) and in chrome. If things aren't working as intended this is likely the issue.
